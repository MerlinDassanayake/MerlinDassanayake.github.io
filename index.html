Soybean Project

This project focuses on predicting soybean grain yield (GY) using machine learning models. The dataset, sourced from a study by de Oliveira et al. (2023), includes 40 soybean cultivars and various farming conditions. The goal was to build predictive models to help farmers optimize crop yield by identifying key factors such as cultivar type, plant height, and pod insertion.
